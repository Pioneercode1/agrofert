Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Are your products safe for organic farming?

Yes, many of our products, specifically the BioVital line, are certified organic. We strictly adhere to international organic farming standards. Please check the specific product label or datasheet for certification details.



Do you offer international shipping?

Absolutely. We currently export to over 20 countries. Our logistics team ensures that all shipments comply with international agricultural transport regulations and arrive safely and on time.



How can I become a distributor?

We are always looking for partners who share our vision. Please visit our Contact page and select "Distributor" in the role field. Our partnership team will review your application and get back to you within 3 business days.



What is the shelf life of your liquid fertilizers?

Our liquid fertilizers typically have a shelf life of 2-3 years when stored correctly in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Expiry dates are clearly printed on all packaging.



Where can I find Safety Data Sheets (SDS)?

Safety Data Sheets are available for download on each individual product page. If you need a specific document that is not listed, please contact our support team.
